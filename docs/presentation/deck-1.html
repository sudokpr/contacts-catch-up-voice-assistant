AI Voice Relationship Manager

Maintaining and strengthening business relationships at scale through intelligent voice interactions

Problem

  • Business relationships weaken without consistent touchpoints
  • Follow-ups are manual and often missed
  • CRMs track data but don’t build relationships
  • High-value partners receive low-frequency attention

Result: Lost goodwill, missed opportunities

Solution

  • Proactively reaches out to partners
  • Reacts to events, signals, and gaps
  • Converses naturally over voice
  • Remembers context across interactions

Feels human, not automated

Core Business Use Cases

  • Festive outreach: Deepavali, New Year wishes
  • Relationship nurturing: periodic check-ins
  • Deal follow-ups: post-closure engagement
  • Gap detection: reach out after inactivity
  • Smart callbacks: “call me tomorrow evening”

Example: Deepavali Outreach

  • Send sweets or gifts
  • Assistant follows up with a call

“Hi Rajesh, wishing you and your family a very happy Deepavali! Hope you received the sweets.”

Builds goodwill at scale

What Makes This Different

Traditional CRM

  • Tracks data
  • Sets reminders
  • Requires manual action

This System

  • Acts on your behalf
  • Initiates conversations
  • Uses memory for context

System Architecture

  • Memory (Qdrant): stores highlights, context, past interactions
  • Trigger Engine: decides when to act
  • Voice Layer (Vapi): handles natural conversations
  • LLM Layer: extracts summaries, facts, callbacks

Memory & Context

  • Stores key information from conversations
  • Uses semantic search to retrieve relevant context

“Last time we spoke about your expansion — how’s that going?”

Trigger Engine

  • Time-based: scheduled outreach
  • Event-based: deal closed, milestone
  • Inactivity: relationship gap detection
  • Callbacks: “call me later”

End-to-End Flow

Event / Signal
      ↓
Trigger Engine
      ↓
Voice Call
      ↓
Conversation + Context
      ↓
Memory Update
      ↓
Next interaction improves
